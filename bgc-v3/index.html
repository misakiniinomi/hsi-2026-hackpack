The Challenge

Good Programs. Empty Seats.

✓ What the Club has
  • 🏆 Life-changing programs — CareerLaunch, robotics, sports teams
  • 📜 Real certifications: OSHA 10, ServSafe Food Handler, CPR
  • 💼 Actual job placements through club partnerships
  • 🏟️ 40,000 sq ft facility, dedicated staff, 5-day programming
⚠ What it's missing
  • 📉 After-school, athletics & teen enrollment declining for 2 years
  • 📣 No way to tell its story — no marketing budget, no dedicated outreach staff
  • 🖥️ Only tool: Canva. Result: occasional, amateur flyers
  • 😶 225 kids participated last year — but never became members
The barrier is not the $50. The barrier is invisibility.

Constraints

Directions

Three Ways to Tackle This

★ Recommended
A

"A Day in the Life" Experience

An interactive experience that lets a kid or parent explore what actually happens at B&G Club — hour by hour, program by program. Mobile-first. Personalized. "What would you be doing at 4:30pm on Tuesday?"

B

The $50 Value Calculator

A tool for parents that shows the concrete value of membership — certifications earned, meals provided, jobs connected, college prep. What does $50 actually buy your kid?

C

AI Outreach Kit for Staff

Staff inputs basic event info → AI outputs an Instagram post, TikTok script, school flyer, and parent email. No marketing skill needed. Replaces Canva-only workflow.

💡 Directions can be combined. Direction C works well as a stretch goal alongside Direction A.

FAQ

Quick Answers

Can we change the sign-up page?
No. The national enrollment page is locked — it cannot be modified.
Can we edit the local Club page?
Yes, slightly. Minor text and image edits are possible on the local page.
Is there a budget?
No. Solutions must be zero-cost to build and maintain.
Who is the target audience?
Elementary and middle school kids — or their parents. Both are valid targets.
Who should be able to use the solution?
Club leaders and coaches — even without marketing skills. Simpler is better.
What kind of proposals are most welcome?
Small things that can start immediately and are actually usable in the field. Not elaborate systems.
Can families sign up by phone?
Yes, though not ideal. Phone enrollment is possible as a fallback.
Tips

Before You Start

🗣️
Talk to Kelly or Denise first. 10 minutes with the on-site experts beats 2 hours of assumptions.
🎯
Pick one persona and go deep. A 10-year-old vs. a 16-year-old is a completely different experience.
📱
Mobile-first. Most Rainier Valley families access the internet on their phone.
🏆
Show, don't tell. "We have robotics" means nothing. A story about a specific kid means everything.
✨
Don't build a brochure. Static info isn't an experience. Add interaction, personalization, or surprise.
