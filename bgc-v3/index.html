The Challenge
Good Programs. Empty Seats.
3 / 9

What's going wrong

  • After-school & teen enrollment declining for 2 years
  • 4 competitors within 2.5 miles — 3 are completely free
  • YMCA has school partnerships — gets chosen automatically
  • Only marketing tool: Canva. No budget, 1 person.
  • 225 kids participated last year but never became members

What the Club has (but can't show)

  • Life-changing programs — CareerLaunch, robotics, sports
  • Real certifications: OSHA 10, ServSafe, CPR
  • Dedicated, passionate staff
  • 24 members enrolled in 4-year universities after graduating
The barrier isn't the $50.
The barrier is invisibility.

Mentor Tips

🗣️
Talk to Kelly or Denise first. 10 minutes with the on-site experts beats 2 hours of assumptions.
🎯
Pick one persona and go deep. A 10-year-old vs. a 16-year-old is a completely different experience.
📱
Mobile-first. Most Rainier Valley families use the internet on their phone.
🏆
Show, don't tell. "We have robotics" means nothing. A specific story about a real kid means everything.
